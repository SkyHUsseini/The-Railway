Gotthard Bernina Circular Tour

A world of blue and white all in one day

The Gotthard Bernina circular tour the best the Swiss Railway network has to offer: from Zurich to Gotthard base tunnel to Lugano, where Bernina Express Bus to Tirano already awaits.

Enjoy pizza and ice-cream before continuing your journey abroad the Bernina Express along the spectacualr UNESCO World Heritage tour to Chur.

Round it all off by enjoying the relaxing last strech of the journey of the panoramic car past Lake Walensee.
